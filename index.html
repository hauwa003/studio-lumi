Studio policies Both

Working hours

Core hours 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM (WAT), Monday through Friday. Async work is flexible outside these hours.

Communication

Client-facing: respond within 24 hours on business days. Email for formal communication, Slack for quick syncs.

Confidentiality

All client work, internal processes, and financial data are confidential. Portfolio pieces require a separate signed approval form.

Time off

Two weeks advance notice for planned time off. Cover handoff responsibilities before leave. Emergency leave communicated ASAP.

Design review process Hauwa

Iteration ready
→
Prep presentation
→
Present to client
→
Collect feedback
→
Implement
→
Final approval
1
Internal review first
Review all screens for consistency, spacing, and edge cases before sharing with client.
2
Prepare client presentation
Create a dedicated review page in Figma with annotations explaining design decisions.
3
Present to client
Schedule a live walkthrough or send an async Loom recording. Never send raw Figma links without context.
4
Collect structured feedback
Use the feedback form template. Client has 3 business days to consolidate feedback.
5
Implement revisions
Address feedback in a new iteration branch. Track changes in the revision log.
6
Final approval
Get written sign-off via email or form before moving to handoff.
Default: 2 revision rounds included. Additional rounds billed at the hourly rate in the contract.

Version control
Increment version on major changes. Archive old versions, never delete them.

New client process Hauwa

1
Lead inquiry received
Client reaches out via contact form, DM, or email. Log immediately in leads tracker.
2
Discovery call (30 min)
Understand project scope, timeline, budget, and fit. Send Loom recap after if helpful.
3
Internal alignment
Discuss with Maryam. Agree on pricing, timeline, and who owns the project.
4
Send proposal + contract
Scoped proposal with pricing, timeline, and deliverables. Client signs contract and pays 50% deposit.
5
Send intake form
Client fills out detailed intake form covering brand, goals, references, and tech stack.
6
Admin setup
Create project folder, set up in Linear, create Figma file, set up Slack channel, send welcome email.
7
Project kickoff
Kickoff call or Loom. Share timeline, communication norms, revision process, and workspace access.
